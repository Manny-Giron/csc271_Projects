What are we?

Arms & Range Buddy is your all-in-one shooting companion.
Whether you’re a beginner learning the basics or an experienced shooter tracking years of practice, this web app helps you stay organized, safe, and connected.
With tools for logging range sessions, managing your collection, reinforcing safety, and sharing with friends, it provides everything you need to track progress and enjoy your shooting journey—all from one secure, cloud-based platform.

What do we provide?

  • Range Day Logging
  • Firearm Collection Management
  • Safety & Training Resources
  • Cloud Storage
  • Social Feature
