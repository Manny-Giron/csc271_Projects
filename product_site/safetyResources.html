Firearm Safety Resources

Firearm Safety Rules

Always treat every firearm as if it is loaded. Never point a firearm at anything you do not intend to shoot. Keep your finger off the trigger until you are ready to shoot. Be sure of your target and what is beyond it.

Range Etiquette

Follow all range rules and commands from range officers. Wear appropriate safety gear, including eye and ear protection. Always clean up after yourself and respect other shooters' space.

Safe Storage Practices

Store firearms unloaded in a locked safe or cabinet. Use gun locks for added security. Keep ammunition stored separately from firearms.
